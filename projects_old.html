Pseudo Random Number Generator

This project explored pseudo-random number generation in cryptography, focusing on the risks of weak randomness and predictable seeds. It involved analyzing entropy sources in a Ubuntu 20.4 VM environment, comparing the behavior of /dev/random and /dev/urandom, and demonstrating how poor random number generation can lead to key recovery attacks.
